Privacy Policy

Servera — Remote Server Control  |  Last updated: May 18, 2026

Servera does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All connection data is stored exclusively on your device.

1. About This Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how the Servera application ("App", "we", "our") handles information when you use our Android application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store on remote servers, or share:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Server credentials, passwords, or SSH keys you enter in the App
  • IP addresses or hostnames of servers you connect to
  • Commands you execute on remote servers
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

To provide its core functionality, the App stores the following data only on your device, using Android's local storage:

  • Saved server profiles (hostname, port, username)
  • App preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device through our App. You can delete it at any time by clearing the App's data in your Android settings or by uninstalling the App.

4. Network Connections

The App establishes direct network connections between your device and the remote servers you configure. These connections are made solely to carry out the commands you initiate. We have no visibility into, and do not log, any data transmitted over these connections.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting SDKs that would collect user data. The App is distributed via Google Play; Google's own privacy policy applies to the distribution platform.

6. Children's Privacy

Servera is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can address the situation.

7. Security

Because no personal data is transmitted to our servers, there is no remote database that could be breached. We encourage you to secure your device with a lock screen and to use strong credentials when configuring server connections.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
